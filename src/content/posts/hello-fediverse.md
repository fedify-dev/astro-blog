Welcome to this federated blog example! This blog is built with
[Astro](https://astro.build/) and [Fedify](https://fedify.dev/),
two tools that complement each other nicely:

- **Astro** turns Markdown files into a fast static website.
- **Fedify** adds ActivityPub federation, so people on Mastodon and other
  fediverse platforms can follow the blog and receive new posts automatically.

## Following this blog

If you're on Mastodon (or any ActivityPub-compatible platform), you can follow
this blog just like you'd follow any other account. Search for the blog's handle
in your fediverse client and hit *Follow*.

When new posts are published and the blog is redeployed, your timeline will
automatically receive them.
